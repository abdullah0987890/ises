Operations and Maintenance

ICON ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS (IES)

Provides comprehensive services for the Operation and Maintenance of advanced Weighing Systems and Electronic Toll & Toll Management Systems.

These O&M contracts may be executed as part of an integrated Supply, Installation, Commissioning, Testing (SITC) + O&M package, or independently, depending on the project's overarching contractual framework.

Our Services Include:

  • Operation and Management of Permanent and Mobile Weigh Stations: Expertise in operating and managing both fixed and portable weigh stations to ensure accuracy and efficiency.
  • Fine Collection Operations on National Highways and Motorways: Seamless management of fine collection processes to streamline highway and motorway operations.
  • Provision of Technical Support at ETTM Toll Plazas and Operations Centers: Professional technical assistance for the smooth operation of ETTM toll plazas on National Highways, including the Operations Center at NHA Headquarters.
  • Maintenance of Weighing Systems on National Highways and Motorways: Offering ongoing technical support for the maintenance and upkeep of static, mobile, and SSWIM weigh stations across highways and motorways.
